My next Steps

Even though I have learned a lot in the past year, my journey is not over yet. The most difficult part is still the transfer of knowledge from theory to practice. That's why I learn best by doing my own project and incorporating my own ideas and methods. I am currently doing the Data Science Nanodegree at Udacity. It is a very powerful resource to gain and train more than just the basic skills that every data scientist needs to master. More information about the programme and its curriculum can be found here.

In addition to a detailed Github repository, I like to keep my learnings in articles (on Medium) to summarise the most important steps. Reviewing previously learned topics is also a great way to reinforce all the content.

I am currently working on a recommendation engine for users that interact with articles on the IBM Watson Studio platform. The main steps involved are:

  • exploratory data analysis
  • rank based recommendation
  • collaborative filtering
  • content based recommendation
  • matrix factorization
